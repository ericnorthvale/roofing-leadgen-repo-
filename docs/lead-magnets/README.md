# Lead magnets

Email-attachable guides that sit behind a form or are sent as a nurture-step attachment. Each magnet is a single-purpose asset — if it needs a second topic, it becomes a second magnet.

## Live / planned

- `roof-inspection-checklist.pdf` — 2-page homeowner walkthrough; what to look at, what to photograph. **Planned.**
- `insurance-claim-playbook.pdf` — 4-page claim process, what to say to your adjuster, what not to say, what to document. **Planned.**
- `hoa-shingle-matcher.pdf` — per-community approved shingle reference for Bridgeland, Towne Lake, Alden Bridge, Timarron, Creekside Park, Carlton Woods. **Planned.**
- `class-4-ir-math-sheet.pdf` — one-page worksheet with the carrier-discount inputs a homeowner needs to run the Class-4 IR payback math. **Planned.**
- `storm-48-hour-guide.pdf` — what to do in the first 48 hours after a hail or wind event. Not-door-knocking explainer included. **Planned.**

## Accessibility

Every PDF must ship with a properly-tagged structure (headings, alt text on images, reading order). A parallel accessible-HTML version lives at the same URL stem, e.g., `/lead-magnets/roof-inspection-checklist` serves HTML; appending `.pdf` gets the PDF. Counsel review on the insurance-claim + § 27.02 language before shipping.
