Recommend filing a **Section 1(b) intent-to-use application** ($350/class via TEAS Standard) immediately after the LLC is registered, so priority date is as early as possible. Opposition window 30 days after publication; expected registration timeline 8–14 months.

Supplemental recommendations:
1. **LLC first** — file `Northvale Roofing LLC` (or a parent holding name like `Northvale Home Services LLC` for future expansion) with TX Secretary of State before the TM application; LLC ownership is cleaner for the trademark owner line.
