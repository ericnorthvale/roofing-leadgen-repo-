This is a placeholder post. It sits in draft status so the listing page has a real page to build against. Our content pipeline (see `.github/workflows/content-pipeline.yml`) will populate this directory weekly with drafts a human reviews before flipping to `published`.

Two things this blog will do:

- **Useful to the homeowner.** Every post answers a real question we get on the phone or at the inspection: "Is a Class-4 shingle worth it?", "My ridge caps lifted — repair or replace?", "Adjuster's coming — what do I do first?"
- **Specific to NW Houston.** Creekside Park, Bridgeland, April Sound, Magnolia — the posts name real places, real HOAs, real storm windows.

What this blog won't do: ranking-farm keyword-stuffed "5 Best Roofers" listicles. Those don't help you.
